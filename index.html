UPCOMING RUNS

WELCOME

Welcome to the Screaming Achilles Running Group! We have monthly group runs hosted in different areas in the metro Atlanta area. Each month, a volunteer host chooses the route in their part of town, the meetup location, and the post-run brunch spot. If you're a fairly new runner ( or even a walker ) feel free to join in the fun! We will have pace groups determined by who shows up; no one will get left behind or have to go it alone. Pups and jogging strollers are welcomed!

Take the Latest Doodle Poll!

Take the latest poll on Doodle to have a say on when our next Screaming Achilles run will be. Before the month is up, we will close the poll and post the exact date and time for our run.
